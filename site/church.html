Stake Calendar Ongoing

Two documents: Annual stake calendar (list form) and Sunday calendar (table — unit conferences + major Sunday meetings). Both in Google Drive → Stake Presidency → Administration.

  1. Publish all items on the LCR church calendar
  2. Check regularly with RS/Youth/Primary/Activities Committee for event details
  3. Prepare template for Presidency review — they need 3–4 months lead time before publishing to stake leaders
Unit Conferences Per schedule
  1. Pres. Warnick sends the schedule email to unit leaders at the start of the year
  2. Two weeks before each: send reminder to unit + stake leaders, including a copy of Pres. Warnick's original instructions
  3. Before sending the reminder: help schedule a one-on-one between Pres. Warnick and the Bishop/Branch President
  4. You are not required to attend
Protecting Children & Youth (PCY) Training Quarterly review
  1. LCR → Reports → "Protecting Children & Youth Training"
  2. Review quarterly — check completion status for all units in the stake
  3. Communicate status to Unit Executive Secretaries
  4. Send reminders to stake leaders when training has expired or is expiring
Scheduling As needed

Help coordinate appointments for Pres. Warnick. He is typically flexible with members. Be available as a scheduling liaison.
